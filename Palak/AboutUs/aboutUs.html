About Study Befikar

Study Befikar is a modern educational consulting firm based in Mumbai. Although newly established, our team carries 15-25 years of collective experience in the education sector.

We offer a comprehensive suite of services, from profile evaluation and counseling to visa assistance, financial aid, and secure student housing abroad.

Expert Team

Our team brings together 15–25 years of combined expertise, guiding students with knowledge, care, and proven strategies.

Global Reach

We connect students with leading universities and programs across the globe, making study abroad dreams a reality.

Complete Support

From career counseling to admissions, applications, and visa guidance—we provide step-by-step assistance at every stage.

Proven Success

Thousands of students nationwide have achieved their education goals with our reliable and successful guidance.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality international education by providing personalized, comprehensive guidance to students from all backgrounds. We strive to bridge the gap between dreams and reality, making study abroad opportunities accessible to every deserving student.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and innovative educational consulting firm, recognized for our commitment to student success and our ability to transform educational aspirations into life-changing opportunities across the globe.

Meet Our Founder and CEO

Harshit Kumar

His journey commenced as a seeker of knowledge, just like many of us. Hailing from a business family in Rajasthan, he too dreamt of exploring the world through education. However, nestled in a Tier 2 City, he encountered the formidable challenge that countless students face - the quest for the finest study abroad consultants in India.

Jaipur, his beloved hometown, seemed like an uncharted territory, devoid of the guidance he needed. In the labyrinth of 2016, finding a mentor for his study abroad voyage was a formidable task, and the expertise he sought remained elusive. Fueled by this struggle, Harshit embarked on a mission to forge a solution.
