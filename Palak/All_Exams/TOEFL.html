Your Guide to TOEFL iBT Success

The TOEFL iBT (Internet-Based Test) is a globally recognized exam assessing your academic English skills in Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing. It’s accepted by thousands of universities and institutions worldwide.

As of 2025, the TOEFL iBT is shorter and more focused, with a total score out of 120. Starting Jan 21, 2026, it’s getting updates to better align with modern academic needs.

Four sections: Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing

Total test duration: approx. 2 hours (116 minutes)

Each section scored 0-30; total score 0-120

New scoring scale (1-6 in 0.5 increments) will be introduced from Jan 21, 2026 alongside current scale during transition period

TOEFL Exam Pattern & Scoring System

Understanding the TOEFL format helps you prepare effectively and achieve your target score for university admissions worldwide.

Reading

35 minutes | 20 questions

Academic passages with comprehension questions to test reading and analysis skills.

Listening

36 minutes | 28 questions

Conversations and lectures with questions on understanding main ideas, details, and inferences.

Speaking

16 minutes | 4 tasks

Express opinions on familiar topics and integrate reading & listening into spoken responses.

Writing

29 minutes | 2 tasks

Write essay responses based on reading/listening materials and express clear, well-structured arguments.

120

Maximum Score

  • Total score range: 0–120

TOEFL Exam Dates

The TOEFL iBT exam is offered frequently throughout the year, both at authorized test centers and at home (TOEFL iBT Home Edition). Candidates should register early to secure their preferred date and location.

Test Type
Availability / Window
Notes / Registration Info
TOEFL iBT (Test Center)
Available on fixed dates, over 60 times a year worldwide
Conducted at ETS-authorized test centers. Seats may fill quickly during peak admission seasons.
TOEFL iBT Home Edition
Available 24/7, appointments 24 hours after registration
Can be taken at home with online proctoring. Requires a quiet environment and system check before the test.
TOEFL Paper Edition
Offered a few times a year in select countries
Reading, Listening & Writing at test center; Speaking completed at home via online session.
Retakes / Attempts
No official attempt limit
Students must wait at least 3 days before retaking TOEFL. Recommended to plan strategically for application deadlines.
