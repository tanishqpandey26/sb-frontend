Your Guide to PTE Academic Success

The Pearson Test of English Academic (PTE Academic) is a widely recognized English language test for study, work, and migration purposes. Accepted by thousands of universities and institutions worldwide, it evaluates your English proficiency in Listening, Reading, Speaking, and Writing.

The exam is entirely computer-based, providing fast results. Scores range from 10–90 and are valid for 2 years.

Four sections: Listening, Reading, Speaking & Writing

Total test duration: Approximately 3 hours

Scoring scale: 10–90 (valid for 2 years)

Test Format

Speaking & Writing | Reading | Listening

Scoring System

10–90 overall score | Automated scoring for most tasks

Duration

Approximately 2 hours (computer-based, no scheduled breaks)

PTE Academic Exam Pattern & Scoring System

Understanding the PTE Academic format helps you prepare effectively and maximise your overall score.

Speaking & Writing

77–93 minutes

Includes personal introduction, read aloud, repeat sentence, describe image, summarize written text, essay writing; assesses pronunciation, fluency, grammar, vocabulary, written content.

Reading

32–41 minutes

Multiple-choice, re-order paragraphs, fill in blanks; evaluates comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and ability to follow text structure.

Listening

45–57 minutes

Summarize spoken text, multiple-choice questions, fill in blanks, highlight correct summary; tests understanding of spoken English, note-taking, and grammar.

90

Maximum Score

  • Each section scored 10–90
  • Automated scoring for most tasks ensures quick results
  • Overall score is based on combined performance across Speaking, Writing, Reading & Listening
Note: PTE Academic is fully computer-based; all sections are completed in one session without breaks.
